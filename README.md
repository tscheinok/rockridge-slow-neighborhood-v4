# Rockridge Slow Neighborhood

A community advocacy website for traffic calming in Oakland's Lower Rockridge neighborhood.

## About

This single-page website presents data and evidence supporting traffic calming measures (diverters) to reduce cut-through traffic on residential streets in the Rockridge "trapezoid" area of Oakland, CA.

## Live Site

Visit the site at: [https://tscheinok.github.io/rockridge-slow-neighborhood/](https://tscheinok.github.io/rockridge-slow-neighborhood/)

## Data Sources

- UC Berkeley Institute of Transportation Studies
- Oakland Department of Transportation (OakDOT)
- Federal Highway Administration (FHWA) standards
- Fehr & Peers CEQA study
